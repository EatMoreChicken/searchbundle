How it works
Built for people who have a plan, not a spreadsheet addiction.
SearchBundle isn't a budgeting app. It's a checkpoint for your financial life. Come in periodically, update your numbers, and let Cooper help you stay on course.

Periodic Check-ins

Monthly, quarterly, or annual. The app walks you through every account with a TurboTax-style guided flow. Five minutes and you're done.

Forward Projections

See where your money is headed in 5, 10, or 40 years. Adjust growth rates, contributions, and timelines in real time.

Cooper, Your AI Guide

Ask anything about your finances. Run scenarios, get suggestions, or learn the basics. Cooper knows your data and speaks plainly.

Manual by Design

No bank linking. No credentials. You enter your balances, which builds awareness and keeps your data private and portable.

On-Track Indicators

Every account with a plan gets a green, yellow, or red status. At a glance, you know if you need to adjust.

Notes & Journal

Annotate your accounts with context. "Refinanced in March." "Employer match went up." Cooper can reference your notes later.

The Check-in Flow
Five minutes. Every account. Done.
No transaction categorization. No receipt scanning. Just update your balances and let SearchBundle handle the rest.
01

Welcome Back

See how long it's been since your last check-in, a summary of what's changed, and what to expect in this session.

02

Account Walk-Through

One account at a time. Pre-filled with your last balance. Just confirm or update. Add a note if something changed.

03

Change Detection

SearchBundle flags anything surprising. A big drop, a faster-than-expected payoff. You decide if it needs attention.

04

Cooper Debrief

At the end, Cooper summarizes the session: net worth change, goal progress, and any suggestions for your next move.
